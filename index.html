Final Year Project: Virtual News Aggregator

In my Final Year Project, "Virtual News Aggregator," I played a pivotal role as a full-stack developer, taking charge of both front-end and back-end development to craft a seamless news-sharing system. This project was aimed to develop a global news-sharing system that virtualizes the process, offering users the convenience of accessing top news platforms with just a single click. The system provides users with the ability to view news from diverse platforms in a centralized and organized manner, enhancing usability. Users can easily switch between platforms and access news from different sources without any barriers. Implemented a feature that allows users to select news categories using voice commands, in addition to manual input, resulting in relevant news articles being displayed. User feedback is welcomed and taken into consideration. Emphasized data security features to ensure a secure news portal. The system aims to provide an efficient and user-friendly experience for accessing news from multiple sources.

Travel Login Management System

The project aims to enhance a travel agency's operational system, which handles various tasks and processes, including customer information. The objective is to develop a highly automated system that streamlines and offers flexibility in travel activities. Users will have access to accurate and timely information, such as hotel and vehicle details, prior to their trips. Proposed Features: Special Discount Offers: Regular customers who frequently use the travel agency's services can enjoy discounts. The Database Administrator (DBA) maintains a list of these customers. Low, Medium, High Package Offers: Customers can choose a package based on their preferences, and the services provided will correspond accordingly. The level of services offered varies depending on the selected package (low, medium, high). Payment Refund (deduction): In case of trip cancellations due to emergencies, customers are eligible for a refund. The DBA configures the system to deduct a specific amount from the payment, and the remaining amount is refunded. The deduction amount is determined by the customer's chosen package (low, medium, high).
